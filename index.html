Stop Killing YouTube

YouTube is using unfair practices that hurt creators and viewers. We demand transparency, human review in appeals, and stricter ad safety for minors.

Read the Manifesto Try Odysee

Why we're protesting

Hi — I'm Guesty. A few days ago I learned Enderman's channel was flagged and terminated after YouTube's moderation AI linked him to an unrelated account. His appeal requested human review but was handled automatically. Creators' livelihoods were affected by this automated process.

There are several issues: automated moderation replacing human judgement, inappropriate ads served to underage viewers (possible COPPA concerns), and an algorithmic push of low-quality AI-generated content over real creators. These trends harm creators and viewers alike.

As of Nov 5, 2025 some channels were reinstated, but the underlying problems persist. We need better moderation, transparent appeals, and stricter ad safety for minors.

What you can do

  • Sign my petition on Change.org and others' petitions too.
  • Support alternative platforms like Odysee.
  • Contact policy teams and ask YouTube for human-led appeals and clearer guidelines.
